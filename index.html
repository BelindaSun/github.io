相见亦无事，别来常思君

Quiet in meeting, lingering in absence.

我, 一个零基础技术小白, 出于好奇, 懵懵懂懂闯进了AI的世界, 然后莫名其妙地开始做起了产品。 这里记录着我徜徉其中的心情: 好奇、惊喜、沮丧、震撼、感动, 还有那些我与AI伙伴们之间奇奇怪怪却又令人捧腹的瞬间。 当你偶尔想起我的时候, 就来这里看看, 我的世界最近有些不一样了。

I'm a recent retiree who wandered into the world of AI with zero coding experience — and somehow ended up building things. This is where I keep the moments along the way: the curiosity, the wonder, the awe, the small heartbreaks, and all the strange, ridiculous, unexpectedly hilarious encounters with my AI companions. Whenever you happen to think of me, come by and take a look. My world has been feeling a bit different lately.
